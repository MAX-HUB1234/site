About the BMW M Series

The BMW M Series brings motorsport performance to the road. Each model blends luxury, power, and track-inspired design to deliver an unmatched driving experience.

BMW M2

A compact beast with sharp handling and aggressive styling.

Engine: 3.0L TwinPower Turbo I6
Horsepower: Up to 453 hp
0–100 km/h: ~4.1 seconds

BMW M3

The iconic performance sedan—sporty, bold, and versatile.

Engine: 3.0L TwinPower Turbo I6
Horsepower: Up to 503 hp
0–100 km/h: ~3.5 seconds

BMW M4

A stylish coupe with serious track-ready performance.

Engine: 3.0L TwinPower Turbo I6
Horsepower: Up to 543 hp
0–100 km/h: ~3.7 seconds

BMW M5

Luxury and performance collide in this executive rocket.

Engine: 4.4L TwinPower Turbo V8
Horsepower: Up to 617 hp
0–100 km/h: ~3.3 seconds
